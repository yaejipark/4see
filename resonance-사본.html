Doll Roll Art Therapy Kit with Mom

Art therapy should be done 10-20 times to see the effect. That is why it is difficult for economically difficult children to use. The art therapy kit consists of crayons, sketchbooks, dolls and guidebooks. At the age of four, a child begins to have secrets. When mother and child play art together, it is easier to talk about dolls. Crayons used to draw on dolls are erased when washed. So you can use it many times.
